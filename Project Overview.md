# Talent Hub

## Overview

Talent Hub is a secure enterprise freelancing and workforce management platform designed to connect clients and freelancers through a structured hiring and collaboration ecosystem. The platform enables businesses to post contracts, hire skilled freelancers, monitor work progress, manage secure milestone-based payments, conduct video meetings, and track productivity through intelligent workflow tools.

Freelancers can showcase portfolios, upload project history, manage contracts, and collaborate directly with clients in a transparent environment. Talent Hub focuses on protecting both clients and freelancers by introducing secure payment workflows, attendance verification systems, AI-powered talent recommendations, and real-time progress tracking.

The platform is built for startups, agencies, enterprises, remote teams, and independent professionals who require a centralized and trustworthy workspace for hiring, collaboration, productivity tracking, and project delivery.

---

# Goals

1. Build a secure enterprise freelancing ecosystem that protects both clients and freelancers during hiring, collaboration, and payments.
2. Simplify project management through centralized communication, meetings, contracts, attendance tracking, and workflow monitoring.
3. Provide transparent milestone-based payment systems that ensure freelancers are paid fairly for verified work progress.
4. Improve hiring efficiency using AI-powered talent recommendations and intelligent freelancer shortlisting.
5. Create a scalable digital workspace for remote collaboration, workforce management, and productivity monitoring.

---

# Core User Flow

1. Client registers and creates a business profile.
2. Client posts a contract or project requirement.
3. Freelancers browse contracts and apply using their portfolio and work history.
4. AI-assisted recommendation system helps clients shortlist relevant freelancers.
5. Client reviews freelancer profiles, project history, portfolios, and skills.
6. Client hires a freelancer and initiates a secure contract workflow.
7. Freelancer starts working and logs attendance using face recognition verification.
8. Daily work progress, active hours, and task updates are tracked inside the workspace.
9. Clients monitor progress step-by-step through dashboards and reports.
10. Teams collaborate through video meetings, discussions, and shared workspaces.
11. Milestone-based payments are processed securely after progress verification.
12. Projects are completed, reviewed, and stored in freelancer portfolio history.
