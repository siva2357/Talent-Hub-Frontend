# Freelancer User Navbar Requirements

## Module: Find Work

This module helps freelancers discover, track, and manage job opportunities available on the platform.

### Main Menu
- Find Work

### Sub Menu Links

#### 1. Find Work
**Purpose**
- Browse available jobs and opportunities posted by clients.

**Features**
- Search jobs by keyword
- Filter jobs by category, budget, experience level, and location
- View detailed job descriptions
- Apply to jobs/contracts
- Save jobs for later review

---

#### 2. Saved Jobs
**Purpose**
- Manage bookmarked or saved job opportunities.

**Features**
- View all saved jobs
- Remove saved jobs
- Quick access to job details
- Apply directly from saved list

---

#### 3. Proposals 
**Purpose**
- Track all submitted proposals and applications.

**Features**
- View submitted proposals
- Track proposal status
- Edit or withdraw proposals
- View client responses

---

#### 4. Offers
**Purpose**
- Manage offers received from clients.

**Features**
- View received offers
- Accept or reject offers
- Review contract terms
- Track pending offers

---

# Module: Contract Management

This module allows freelancers to monitor and manage all active contracts and work activities.

## Main Menu
- Contract Management

### Sub Menu Links

#### 1. Your Active Contracts
**Purpose**
- View and manage ongoing contracts.

**Features**
- List all active contracts
- View contract details
- Track deadlines and milestones
- Submit deliverables
- Monitor contract status

---

#### 2. Contract Diary
**Purpose**
- Maintain daily work records and activity logs.

**Features**
- Add daily work updates
- Record completed tasks
- Attach notes or files
- Maintain work history
- View previous activity logs

---

### 3. Hourly Work Diary

#### Purpose
Allow freelancers working on hourly contracts to upload and manage daily work progress and tracked activities.

#### Features
- Upload daily work progress
- Add work descriptions and completed tasks
- Track working hours under the contract
- Upload screenshots or proof of work
- Maintain hourly activity records
- View submitted work history
- Monitor total logged hours
- Support client review and verification
- Track project progress under active contracts

---

# Module: Financial Management

This module helps freelancers manage earnings, reports, and withdrawal activities.

## Main Menu
- Financial Management

### Sub Menu Links

#### 1. Finance Overview
**Purpose**
- Display financial summaries and account information.

**Features**
- Total earnings overview
- Pending payments
- Available balance
- Recent transactions

---

#### 2. Your Report
**Purpose**
- Generate and view financial reports.

**Features**
- Monthly earnings reports
- Transaction summaries
- Download financial reports
- View payment history

---

#### 3. Earnings and Withdraw
**Purpose**
- Manage freelancer earnings and withdrawal requests.

**Features**
- View total earnings
- Request withdrawals
- Track withdrawal status
- View withdrawal history
- Manage payment methods
