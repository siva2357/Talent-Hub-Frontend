👋 Our Story

About Talent Hub

Talent Hub is a skill-first job platform that helps job seekers understand their readiness for roles and helps recruiters evaluate talent based on skills and alignment — not guesswork.

What Is Talent Hub?

Talent Hub is a job-focused platform built around resume intelligence, skill analysis, and job readiness insights.

Instead of relying only on keyword-heavy resumes, Talent Hub evaluates skills, experience relevance, and role alignment to support better hiring outcomes.

Who Talent Hub Is For

Job Seekers

Job seekers who want to understand their resume strength, identify skill gaps, and apply to jobs where they have a stronger role fit.

Recruiters & Employers

Recruiters who want clearer talent insights, skill-based alignment, and structured candidate information to support screening and shortlisting.

How Talent Hub Works

For Job Seekers
  • Upload your resume
  • Get ATS-style resume analysis
  • Identify missing or weak skills
  • Receive resume & skill improvement suggestions
  • View job readiness indicators per role
  • Apply to jobs with better alignment
For Recruiters
  • Post jobs with clear skill requirements
  • Review resume quality and skill alignment
  • View talent readiness indicators
  • Shortlist candidates with structured insights
  • Make informed, human-led hiring decisions

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Help people apply smarter and help recruiters hire better through skill-based clarity and transparency.

Vision

A hiring ecosystem where talent is evaluated by skills, readiness, and real potential — not just resumes.
